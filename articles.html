How to create and complete your profile effectively

To get the most personalized job recommendations, ensure your profile is 100% complete. Upload an updated resume, list your key skills, and set your career preferences. Make sure your contact information is accurate and link your LinkedIn or portfolio for added visibility.

Why your resume upload might fail

Resume uploads can fail due to unsupported file formats (ensure it's .pdf or .docx), large file size (limit is 5MB), or special characters. If the issue persists, try renaming the file or clearing your browser cache.

Best practices for employers on candidate filtering

Use candidate filters to narrow down applicants by skill, experience, and location. AI recommendations will highlight top-fit candidates. Always write clear job descriptions, and avoid bias in filter selection to attract diverse talent.

How to reset your password securely

Click on "Forgot Password" from the login page. You'll receive an email with a secure reset link. Make sure your new password is at least 8 characters long and includes a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.

Understanding job recommendation logic

HireSense uses machine learning to match candidates with jobs based on profile data, skills, past job applications, and activity on the platform. The more data you provide, the more accurate the recommendations become.
