Company Overview

HireSense is a leading AI-powered job recommendation platform that bridges the gap between talented candidates and top-tier companies. Our mission is to provide intelligent, transparent, and efficient hiring experiences for both employers and job seekers using state-of-the-art AI technologies.

Fast Facts

  • Founded in 2025, based in Pune, India
  • AI-powered matching engine for job seekers and employers
  • Over 1K+ active job listings
  • Intelligent resume parsing and skill-based filtering
  • Built with privacy, transparency, and inclusion in mind
