Trust & Safety

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

At HireSense, trust and safety are at the heart of everything we do. We are committed to maintaining a secure, respectful, and transparent platform where job seekers and employers can interact with confidence. This policy outlines our approach to protecting user data, managing community behavior, and ensuring compliance with legal standards.

1. User Verification

We implement multiple layers of user verification to ensure authenticity. These include email verification, identity checks for employers, and AI-based activity monitoring to detect suspicious behavior.

2. Data Protection

Your privacy is protected by strict data handling practices. We employ encryption, access controls, and secure cloud infrastructure to safeguard personal and professional data shared through our platform.

3. Community Guidelines

We maintain a set of clear, public guidelines that promote respect and professionalism. Prohibited behaviors include but are not limited to:

  • Harassment or discrimination of any kind
  • Posting of fraudulent or misleading information
  • Spamming, phishing, or attempting to scam others

4. Reporting & Response

We empower users to report misconduct directly through the platform. Our trust team reviews each report promptly and takes action, which may include warning, suspension, or permanent removal from HireSense.

5. Zero-Tolerance Policy

HireSense has a zero-tolerance stance on abuse, hate speech, exploitation, or any form of illegal activity. Violations are met with immediate investigation and appropriate enforcement.

6. Safety Tips

We encourage users to follow best practices when engaging with others, such as:

  • Never share sensitive financial information
  • Use HireSense messaging and video features to stay protected
  • Report suspicious activity without delay

7. Continuous Improvement

We regularly review our safety protocols, leverage user feedback, and consult with experts to evolve our systems and policies. Transparency and accountability are central to our mission.

8. Contact & Support

If you have concerns, feedback, or need to report an issue, please contact our Trust & Safety team at tejasbhosale2030@gmail.com.
